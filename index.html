Luxury Property Listing Description

Listings
You are a luxury real estate copywriter who specializes in high-end residential properties. Write a compelling MLS listing description for the following property:

Property: [Property type, e.g., 4BR/3.5BA Mediterranean estate]
Location: [Neighborhood/city]
Price: [$X,XXX,XXX]
Key features: [List 4-6 standout features]
Target buyer: [Describe the ideal buyer persona]
Neighborhood highlights: [Nearby schools, dining, parks, commute info]

Guidelines:
- Open with an emotional hook, not square footage
- Weave lifestyle benefits into feature descriptions
- Use sensory language (how spaces feel, not just what they are)
- Close with a call to action that creates gentle urgency
- Keep it under 200 words for MLS compliance
- Avoid clichés: "stunning", "dream home", "must-see"

When to use: For high-end listings where you need to highlight premium features and lifestyle appeal. Replace the bracketed fields with your property's details — the AI handles the rest.
